OUR MISSION

We are in a moment when Black women are getting more recognition for their activism and grassroots work. People had written and represented Black women as rank and file but not as intellectuals. We are here to bring our black women into the high society of equity and confidence, by having them embrace the technological world of Coding, NFTs, Blockchain, and Metaverse with our visual representations.

Our goal each year to ensure 10,000 of our Women and girls have enrolled in tech programs and championed in trending tech

A percentage of TBW mint profits will be donated to our partner organization WITI - the leading organization for the advancement and inclusion of women in business and technology with more than 30 years experience to set our Black Women and girls on the path they deserve to be in our tech community.

The Black Woman NFT is the most recent art and Tech Project of Christian Amoh Esq

He is a Ghanaian born and well-established international affairs and integration personnel but believes in art and technology to be the next push of the universe. He has worked with amazing inter and non-intergovernmental organizations such as The European Association for External Relations, Latter Day Saint Charities, International Union of Science and Friendship and Nova Skola.

Christian has seen the gap and underrepresentation of Black Women and women in general in all sectors with huge recess difference in the tech industries, while on his works. He has firm belief that this initiative will bridge the gender gap in our communities where Black women and girls, deprived and marginalized, will be brought out of obscurity to aid push our world of technology to the next level.
